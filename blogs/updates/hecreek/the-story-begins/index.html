HECreek, The story begins!

Aug 24, 2014 - Posted in updates, hecreek

Well since I’m a freelancer now, I decided to create a dedicated website for a new brand sole Proprietorship company to showcase my services and past work I’ve made during the past years.

I went directly to FatWallet & searched for GoDaddy coupons for .com domains and after getting my coupon I went to NameCheckr to see what name could I come up with that is available on all social media accounts as well as a .com domain. and the winner was HECreek…

Instantly I visualized how to logo will look like and how I will be shaping this development studio into a really freelance business. I’ll post here daily my HECreek updates along with tutorials on how I’ll develop and host the new studio website… It should be fun I guess…
