About me

I absolutely love to code!

Hello again, My name is Hashem Zahran, a passionate freelance Web & Mobile UX/UI Developer based in the beautiful mediterranean city of Alexandria, Egypt.

I'm always a visionary entrepreneur at heart with several involvements since my teen years like founding a computer hardware company at the age of 18. I've since co-founded & operated multiple design agencies & worked as a Front-End Developer for several technolgy companies as well.

I do wireframes, prototypes, mockups, & layouts using my favorite design tool "Illustrator" and my favorite editor "Sublime Text". I love developing for WordPress with clear code and creative results. I also help brands in discovering new ways to interact with their customers via latest trends in social media marketing.
